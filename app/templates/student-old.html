How to Participate in Research

Getting Started

To participate, you must have a valid VU Amsterdam student account.

Log in to VBL SONA using VU SSO (Single Sign-On), and participate in the available studies!

When logging in for the first time, fill in your name, email, address, and IBAN (for payment). This information, per VU privacy policy, is only accessed by the financial administration to process payments. Please notify us by email at vbl@vu.nl if any details change.

Paid Research Participation

You will receive an email when a study is available for participation. You may participate in either online or physical lab studies:

  • For online studies, you can participate immediately.
  • For physical lab studies, you must select a timeslot from the available options.

The compensation rate is 12.5 EUR per hour for lab studies and 10 EUR per hour for online studies, with potential performance incentives for some studies. After participating, payment will usually take 2-3 weeks.

Physical study participation may be canceled up to 24 hours in advance. Accumulating three unexcused absences will result in a suspension of your account. To reactivate your account, email us!

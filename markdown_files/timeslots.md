# Create timeslots
Ensure to already have timeslots ready. You can do this by:

- Go to “My Studies” in the menu at the top. You will see all studies (experiments); now click “Time slots” to the right of the study you want to give time slots. You will be shown a display of the current schedule/time slots. If you have not yet posted any timeslot nothing will appear.
- To add one new time slot click the ‘Add A Time slot’ button
Fill in the required information

>[!info] <i class="fa-solid fa-info"></i> &nbsp Conducting Research in the Applied Behavioural Science Labs (Transitorium)
> - If you are conducting your research in the Applied Behavioural Science labs at the Transitorium, please reach out to us at **[vbl@vu.nl](mailto:vbl@vu.nl)** and enquire about the lab space availability on the dates and times that you want to run the experiment. We will advise you the appropriate time window to run your studies.
> - After receiving the available dates and times for the lab, you or your research assistant can create time slots for participants to sign up.
> - On the day of the experiment, pick up students from the waiting room and give them the instructions. Have people sign the attendance for the session by mentioning their anonymous SONA ID.

Please ensure to consider at least 20% drop-out rates. Therefore, open timeslots for the desired number of participants including the potential drop-outs.

We recommend you always add timeslots for a maximum of 50 participants with a 1-week deadline, so that we can process the payments accordingly. If you need to collect more data, you can always add additional timeslots for 50 participants as and when needed.

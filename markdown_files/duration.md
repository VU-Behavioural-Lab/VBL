# Duration
Here, insert the **time (in minutes)** that the study will take participants to complete.

Please round up, e.g., 8 minutes to 10 minutes, as coordinators give research credits in 5-minute increments. Keep in mind that physical sessions take longer than the actual study. Students need time to wait in the waiting room, listen to instructions, and take their places. These activities can easily add another ten minutes to each session, so be sure to account for this in the total duration you choose.

**We strongly advise you to test the duration of your study.** If you have a physical lab study and would like to test it in the lab, please contact us, and we will assist you.

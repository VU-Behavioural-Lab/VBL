
# Study URL and survey setup
{% if study_type == 'online'%}
When filling out the study information, you will be asked to enter a **Study URL**. Before submitting your study, please ensure that your survey meets the requirements below.
{% endif %}

{% if study_type == 'lab'%}
When filling out the study information, you will be asked to enter a **Study URL**. You do not need to add any URL to your lab study as the study will be conducted in the lab.

If you are collecting data using a survey in the lab, please ensure that your survey has proper first pages with the consent form as indicated below.
{% endif %}

## Page 1: Introduction / Instructions
Please ensure the first page of your survey includes all required information:

- Brief study description
- Study duration
- Study language
- Payment information (Payment is processed after the timeslot deadline)
- A statement that incomplete responses will not be compensated
- A statement indicating the presence of attention-check questions (Failed attention checks result in no compensation)
- A statement that there are no right or wrong answers
- A consent statement, e.g., "I have read the instructions carefully and I consent to participate in this study."
- Researcher names and university affiliations

## Page 2: SONA ID
Please add a question asking participants to enter their SONA ID. For clarity, you can use: “Please enter your SONA ID (4 or 5-digit number). This is not your student number.”
